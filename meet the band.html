 Chris

 

Chris plays guitar, bass, and sings for Chriana. He has been playing guitar since he was 17 years old. Chris plays electric and acoustic 6 string guitars and he also plays 12 string guitars. Chris writes most of the music for Chriana and some of the lyrics. Chris used to write and perform music with his friend when he was in his 20s. Music is back in his life through Chriana.
