 Lana

Lana plays drums, keys, bass, and sings for Chriana. She has been playing drums since she was 8 years old. Lana writes most of the lyrics and some music for Chriana. She discovered that music was her passion in her third year of high school. Lana is also a member of YoWo Music where she gets to play in bands and learn about music and song writing.
