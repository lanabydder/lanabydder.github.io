Isolation 2 (2020)

Isolation 2 is Chriana’s debut album. It was created during the 2020 COVID lockdowns, hence the title. It features songs such as Chriana’s hit debut single “I’ll Be Me”.

 

 

 

Hope '21 Remix (2021)

Hope was written by Chris in 1996. It has had many different remixes since then. Its 2020 remix is featured as the final track on Isolation 2. After review, it was decided it needed a 2021 remix.

 

 

May (I) (2021)

May (I) is Chriana’s most recent single which is going to be featured on our second album (which is currently untitled). May (I) was written for Chris’s grandpa on his 100th birthday.
